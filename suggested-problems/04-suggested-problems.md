**Problem 2** Suppose that the CDF of a continuous probability measure $P$ on $S=\mathbb{R}$ is given by

$$
F(s) = \begin{cases}
0 & : s \leq 0, \\
\frac{1}{9}s^2 & : 0 < s \leq 3, \\
1 & : s>3.
\end{cases}
$$

Find and sketch a PDF of the measure $P$.
